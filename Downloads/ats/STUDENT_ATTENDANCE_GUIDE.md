# 📚 Student Attendance Guide - Simple Steps

## How Students Give Attendance

### 🎯 Quick Overview
You need **2 things** to mark attendance:
1. Your **QR Code** (given by admin)
2. Your **Face** (blink detection for security)

---

## 📝 Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Get Your QR Code 🎫
- Your teacher/admin will give you a unique QR code
- It looks like this: ▢▢▢▢ (black and white squares)
- **Print it** or **save on your phone**
- Keep it safe - you'll need it every day!

### Step 2: Approach the Attendance System 🚶
- Go to the computer with webcam
- Stand in front of the camera
- Distance: About 2-3 feet away

### Step 3: Show Your QR Code 📱
- Hold your QR code in front of the camera
- Keep it steady for 2-3 seconds
- The system will scan it automatically

### Step 4: Wait for Your Name 👋
- System will say: **"Good Morning [Your Name]"**
- This means it recognized your QR code
- Now get ready for face verification

### Step 5: Blink Your Eyes 👁️👁️
- System will say: **"Please blink your eyes to confirm your identity"**
- Look directly at the camera
- **Blink your eyes twice** naturally
- Don't blink too fast or too slow

### Step 6: Done! ✅
- System will say: **"Your attendance has been marked"**
- You're all set!
- Go to your class

---

## ⏰ Timing

### Check-In (Morning)
- Mark attendance when you arrive
- System records your **entry time**
- Example: 09:00 AM

### Check-Out (Evening)
- Mark attendance when you leave
- System records your **exit time**
- Example: 05:00 PM

---

## 💡 Tips for Success

### ✅ DO:
- Stand in good lighting
- Look directly at camera
- Remove sunglasses
- Keep face clearly visible
- Blink naturally
- Hold QR code steady

### ❌ DON'T:
- Cover your face with mask/scarf
- Look away from camera
- Blink too fast
- Move around while scanning
- Use someone else's QR code (it won't work!)

---

## 🎭 What If It Doesn't Work?

### QR Code Not Scanning?
- Hold it closer/farther
- Make sure it's not damaged
- Check if it's the right QR code
- Ask teacher for help

### Face Not Recognized?
- Check lighting
- Remove glasses
- Look directly at camera
- Blink again
- Ask teacher to retrain your model

### System Says "Access Denied"?
- Your face doesn't match the database
- Contact admin/teacher
- You may need to retake photos

---

## 🔐 Why Blink Detection?

**Security Reason**: 
- Prevents someone from using your photo
- Ensures you're a real person
- Protects against cheating

**How it works**:
- Camera detects your eye movement
- Calculates eye aspect ratio
- Confirms you're alive and present

---

## 📊 What Gets Recorded?

When you mark attendance, the system saves:
- Your **Employee/Student ID**
- **Date** (today's date)
- **Time** (when you marked)
- **Face verification status** (✅ or ❌)
- **Type** (Check-in or Check-out)

## 🚨 Common Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Not Looking at Camera
**Problem**: Face not detected  
**Solution**: Look directly at the camera lens

### Mistake 2: Blinking Too Fast
**Problem**: Blink not counted  
**Solution**: Blink naturally, like you normally do

### Mistake 3: Poor Lighting
**Problem**: Face not recognized  
**Solution**: Stand where there's good light

### Mistake 4: Covering Face
**Problem**: Face features not visible  
**Solution**: Remove mask, scarf, or hand from face
